# Smart Fuel Management System - Vibeathon Project Brief

## Case Study

### Title
**Know When to Fill Up. Know How to Drive Smart. Save More Every Week.**

### Background

Fuel price adjustments and rising transportation costs continue to affect daily commuters and vehicle owners in Malaysia.

Since fuel prices for certain fuel categories are periodically revised under Malaysia's fuel pricing mechanism, drivers often face difficulties managing fuel expenses efficiently and understanding the impact of their driving habits on both personal finances and environmental sustainability.

---

## Key Challenges

### 1. Limited awareness of fuel price trends and refuelling strategies

Many drivers refuel only when their fuel tanks are nearly empty without monitoring fuel price trends or planning refuelling timing.

As a result, opportunities to reduce transportation expenses through more informed refuelling decisions are often missed.

### 2. Lack of visibility into personal fuel consumption

Most drivers do not actively track how much fuel is consumed during daily travel or how driving behaviors such as:

- Aggressive acceleration
- Long idling periods
- Inefficient route selection

may affect fuel usage and monthly spending.

### 3. Limited understanding of environmental impact

The transportation sector remains one of the major contributors to carbon emissions in Malaysia.

However, individual drivers generally lack accessible tools to estimate their personal transport-related emissions or identify practical ways to reduce fuel consumption and environmental impact.

### 4. Fragmented fuel management practices

Fuel expenses, mileage records, fuel price updates, and driving analytics are often managed separately or not tracked at all.

This makes it difficult for users to gain meaningful insights into their transportation habits.

---

## Problem Summary

These challenges contribute to:

- Higher transportation costs
- Inefficient fuel usage
- Low awareness of sustainable driving practices

---

## Objective

Propose a system that enables intelligent fuel management by:

1. Tracking fuel consumption patterns
2. Analysing driving behaviour
3. Providing insights to optimise refuelling decisions
4. Promoting cost-efficient driving habits
5. Encouraging sustainable transportation practices

---

## Challenge Statement

Design and develop an intelligent fuel management solution that combines fuel tracking, driving analytics, and decision-support insights to help Malaysian drivers reduce transportation costs while promoting fuel efficiency and environmental sustainability.
